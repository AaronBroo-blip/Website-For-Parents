New Parent Guide

Simple routines for first-time moms & dads

Feeding Diapers Swaddling Baby Care

🍼 Feeding Routine

  • Feed every 2–3 hours (or when baby shows hunger cues)
  • Burp halfway through and after feeding
  • Hold baby upright during feeds
  • Throw away unfinished milk after 1 hour

👶 Diaper Changing

  • Change every 2–3 hours or when wet/dirty
  • Wipe front to back
  • Let skin dry before putting on a new diaper
  • Use diaper cream if redness appears

🛏️ Swaddling

  • Use a lightweight blanket
  • Wrap snug but not too tight
  • Make sure hips can move
  • Stop swaddling when baby starts rolling

❤️ General Baby Care

  • Always place baby on their back to sleep
  • Keep sleep area clear (no pillows/toys)
  • Watch for hunger and sleep cues
  • Trust your instincts—you’ll learn your baby
